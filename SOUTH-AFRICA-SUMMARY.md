## 🚀 What You Need to Do Now

### 3 Simple Steps:

#### 1️⃣ **Get a New Meta Pixel for SA** (5 min)
- Go to Meta Events Manager
- Create new pixel: "WaveRide - South Africa"
- Copy the Pixel ID
- Paste it in `index-za.html` (search for `YOUR_ZA_PIXEL_ID_HERE`)

#### 2️⃣ **Create ZAR Product on Selar** (5 min)
- Login to Selar
- Create product priced at R135
- Copy the product link
- Paste it in `index-za.html` (search for `YOUR_SELAR_ZA_LINK_HERE`)

#### 3️⃣ **Deploy the Page** (10 min)
- Upload `index-za.html` to your hosting
- Or deploy to Vercel/GitHub Pages
- Test that it loads correctly

**Total time: ~20 minutes**
